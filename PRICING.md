# KeyHub Central — White-Label Pricing

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** March 2026
**Status:** Active

## Overview

KeyHub Central is licensed as a white-label platform to home service companies, general contractors, staffing agencies, and franchise operations. Companies pay a single monthly or annual fee — all roles (admins, contractors, sales reps, PMs) are included. No per-seat charges.

## What Every Tier Includes

- White-labeled platform — their logo, brand colors, and custom domain
- Full contractor management (profiles, W-9 tracking, insurance, compliance)
- Job pipeline with Kanban board and status tracking
- Role-based access for all user types (admin, contractor, sales rep, PM)
- Performance rating system
- Google Calendar 2-way sync
- AI voice — inbound + outbound (bundled minutes, see below)
- Mobile PWA (contractors install it like an app on iOS/Android)
- Onboarding support

## Setup Fee Rationale

Setup fees scale with tier because the actual onboarding work scales with company size.

| Tier | Setup Fee | What's Included |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Starter | $499 | Branding config, domain setup, 1 admin trained, onboarding call |
| Growth | $1,499 | Above + multi-role setup, 2–3 training sessions |
| Business | $2,999 | Above + data migration, integrations config, workflow setup |
| Scale | $4,999 | Above + dedicated onboarding, multi-location config, SLA |
| Enterprise | Custom | Scoped per deal, dedicated CSM |

> **Close lever:** For annual contracts on Starter/Growth, waiving or discounting the setup fee is an effective incentive that costs very little in real margin.
